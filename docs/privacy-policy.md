# Privacy Policy — Vade

**Last updated:** July 6, 2026

## Summary
Vade is a personal debt/credit tracking app. Your financial data (names, amounts, notes) stays on your device and your personal iCloud account. We do not operate any backend servers that store your financial records.

This policy explains what limited data is collected by the third-party services integrated into the app and how you can control it.

## Data Stored on Your Device
- Contact names and phone numbers (if you choose to import from Contacts)
- Debt and credit records you enter (amounts, dates, notes, payment history)
- App preferences (theme, language, notification settings)

This data is stored locally using Apple's SwiftData framework and synced to your personal iCloud account if you are signed in. We **never** have access to this data.

## Data Collected by Third-Party Services

### Firebase Crashlytics (Crash Reporting)
- **What:** Anonymous technical data when the app crashes (stack traces, device model, OS version)
- **Purpose:** Identify and fix bugs
- **Control:** You can disable this in Settings > Privacy

### Firebase Analytics (Usage Statistics)
- **What:** Anonymous behavioral data (which features are used, how often). **No personal identifiers, names, amounts, or notes are ever sent.**
- **Purpose:** Understand feature usage to improve the app
- **Control:** You can disable this in Settings > Privacy

### Google AdMob (Advertising)
- **What:** Advertising identifier (IDFA) if you grant App Tracking Transparency permission. Non-personalized ads are shown if you decline.
- **Purpose:** Display advertisements to support the free app
- **Control:** You are asked for tracking permission on first launch (Apple's ATT prompt). You can change this anytime in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.

## Data Deletion
You can delete **all** your data at any time from Settings > "Delete All My Data". This permanently removes all records from both your device and iCloud. There is no undo — a double confirmation is required.

## Your Rights
- You can export all your data (PDF/CSV) from within the app at any time
- You can delete all your data with one action
- You can disable analytics and crash reporting independently

## Children's Privacy
This app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
